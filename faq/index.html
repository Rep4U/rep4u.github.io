Frequently Asked Questions

About REP4U?

Real Estate Property For You is a brand new service designed to unload from brokers' back most of the nowadays excessive, unnecessary consuming time activities - allowing them to dedicate those daily hours to more important tasks.

Our Foundations

REP4U.ca foundations are to take out as much as possible the brokers' common wasting time tracking for buyers' answers that will be already available in a database with all already available information.

Where we operate

REP4U.ca first phase will focus Edmonton and Calgary apartment condos only, then gradually expanding to others condos and residential units through out Alberta. And so on till contemplate every residence on Canada.

What do we offer?

REP4U.ca website and database will answer the so frequent questions (1) that today prospect buyers of apartment condos call their brokers, these ones call the seller broker that then calls the seller or the condo manager co. And when the answer is obtained, the phones will ring back… taking time from everyone and taking hours, even days to reach the origin, the interested buyer.

Collection of information

The collection of all information required by REP4U.ca, including all those already on others websites on use by the brokers is logically, friendly prepared on listing forms accessible by brokers members of our website, to be filled right at the moment of his/her meeting with the seller.

Our team

REP4U.ca backoffice team will contact through internet: seller, board of directors, condo manager and every other source available, assuring that pretty much everything expected to be collected will be present in that listing.

Which data do we collect?

Specifics of all kinds will be collected when filling in those forms, and the selections when guided by those filters will allow to much better, directed results. As a conclusion, many less “curious” calling/emailing brokers, and more time for the brokers to attend the real prospect buyers.
